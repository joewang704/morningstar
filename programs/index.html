OUR PROGRAMS

Young Dancer Program

(Beginner - Level 3)


Classes for kids combining movement and music to help students develop body awareness, rhythm, flexibility, coordination, and confidence while learning ballet vocabulary. Beginner is 1 class per week. Starting from Level 1, there will be Chinese Folk class in addition to ballet. Starting Level 3 classes will be TWICE a week, Chinese Folk only on the weekend.

Intermediate Dancer Program

(Level 4 - Level 7)


Structured ballet class focusing on proper posture, technique, and vocabulary with introduction of the use of ballet barre in Level 4. Level 5 students will be introduced to contemporary style dance. Level 5 and above learn 3 styles - Ballet, Folk, Contemporary.
